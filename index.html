Welcome to The Underground

DÜX isn’t a spa. We’re the steel-toed, brick-walled barber’s den tucked under the street — where the music’s good, the fades are cleaner than your search history, and the jokes have actual flavor.

We keep it real, we keep it welcoming, and we keep your lineup razor sharp. No scripts. No upsell speeches. Just barbers who give a damn and hair that behaves even on Monday mornings.

  • Classic cuts, crispy fades, beard wizardry
  • Hot towel shaves & straight razor line work
  • Complimentary chill: water, soda, or “barber’s tea”*

*You bring the bottle. We bring the glasses. Everybody brings the respect.
